About

Coyote Wash Campground is located in a rural neighborhood just outside of Saguaro National Park West. The campground shares property with our home on 1.25 acres that is seperated by a wash allowing privacy. Beautiful views of Kitt Peak and Avra Valley are to the west as well as Tucson Mountain Park to the East.

Other Things to Note

    Please note that our campground accommodates rigs up to 32 feet and requires full self-containment.


    Campground closed End of May - Beginning of September.

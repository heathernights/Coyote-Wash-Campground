Welcome to Coyote Wash Campground

Located 3.8 miles from Saguaro National Park

Coyote Wash Campground, your gateway to the untamed beauty of the Sonoran Desert. Located just a short distance from Saguaro National Park West, our campground offers a serene oasis amidst the rugged desert landscape. Please note that our campground accommodates rigs up to 32 feet and requires full self-containment. While we do not have amenities available on-site, the wonders of the Sonoran Desert await just beyond our doorstep. Come experience the magic of the desert at Coyote Wash Campground, where adventure and tranquility converge in one of the most captivating landscapes on earth. We look forward to welcoming you to our slice of desert paradise.

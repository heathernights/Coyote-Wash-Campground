    Welcome to Coyote Wash Campground, your gateway to the untamed beauty of the Sonoran Desert. Located just 3.8 miles from Saguaro National Park West, our campground offers a serene oasis amidst the rugged desert landscape. As you arrive, you'll be greeted by breathtaking scenery that stretches as far as the eye can see. Settle into your primitive campsite, where fully self-contained adventurers can immerse themselves in the raw beauty of the desert without the distractions of modern amenities. Explore nearby trails and discover the wonders of Saguaro National Park and Tucson Mountain Park, where towering cacti, ancient rock formations, and stunning vistas await your discovery. Whether you're hiking, biking, or simply taking in the sights and sounds of the desert, Coyote Wash Campground provides the perfect base camp for your desert adventures. As night falls, prepare to be dazzled by the brilliance of the desert sky. With minimal light pollution and expansive horizons, the stars shine brighter than ever, casting a magical glow over the desert landscape. Experience the true essence of the desert under the vast expanse of stars at Coyote Wash Campground. Please note that our campground accommodates rigs up to 32 feet and requires full self-containment. While we do not have amenities available on-site, the wonders of the Sonoran Desert await just beyond our doorstep. Come experience the magic of the desert at Coyote Wash Campground, where adventure and tranquility converge in one of the most captivating landscapes on earth. We look forward to welcoming you to our slice of desert paradise.Our 1 acre is sperated by a wash. The other side of that wash is wide open you can choose where to set up camp. There are several flat areas and beautiful views east and west.
